How dangerous is the virus and will my health improve?

According to the data of 56,000 patients, the World Health Organization says that four out of every five people infected with the Corona virus show only slight symptoms, adding:

  • 80 percent have mild symptoms.
  • 14 percent have severe symptoms.
  • 6 percent are seriously ill.

The death rate due to illness appears to be low (between one and two percent), but these rates are not confirmed.
