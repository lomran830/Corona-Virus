The virus is not "new" (used to infect animals), but it is new for humans after it can transmit between different types of organisms.It is not yet known exactly how the Coruna virus spreads from person to person, but similar viruses spread through droplets, such as those produced when a person coughs or sneezes. World health organizations say regular and thorough hand washing is crucial in controlling to avoid infection.

Corona virus can, in severe infections, cause pneumonia, severe acute respiratory syndrome, insufficiency of functions of a number of body parts and even death.

How it spreads?!!

  • There is currently no vaccine to prevent coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19).
  • The best way to prevent illness is to avoid being exposed to this virus.
  • The virus is thought to spread mainly from person-to-person
    • Between people who are in close contact with one another (within about 6 feet).
    • Through respiratory droplets produced when an infected person coughs, sneezes or talks.
    • These droplets can land in the mouths or noses of people who are nearby or possibly be inhaled into the lungs.
    • Some recent studies have suggested that COVID-19 may be spread by people who are not showing symptoms.
